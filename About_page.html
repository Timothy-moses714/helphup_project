About Helphub

Our Mission

Helphub is build to empower communities by connecting people in need with those who can help. From requesting aid to discovering local jobs and urgent SOS support, Helphub brings people together.

What We Do

  • Connect people to offer and recieve help
  • Share rel-time community updates
  • List jobs and volunteer opportunities
  • Enable fast and emergency alerting(SOS)

Our Vision

To create a stronge, more supportive commmunity
network where no one is left behind

About HelpHub

HelpHub connects individuals in communities with local services, jobs, support, and emergency resources. We're here to help you thrive where you live.
